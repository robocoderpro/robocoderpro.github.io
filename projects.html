KUNG FU QUEST

ABU Robocon 2026 (Hong Kong) — "Kung Fu Quest" symbolizes a robotic journey mirroring a disciple's path to mastery. Our dual-robot system must navigate the Martial Club to assemble weapons, traverse the Meihua Forest to collect sacred Kung Fu Scrolls, and engage in a strategic Tic-Tac-Toe showdown in the central Arena to achieve "Kung Fu Master" status.

Autonomous navigation & Tic-Tac-Toe Strategy AI
High-speed Holonomic Drive Systems
Complex Mechanical Weapon Assembly
Real-time Vision Processing for Scroll Detection
