About SPARK

Engineering the Future
of Robotics

SPARK is more than a robotics club — it's a launchpad for the next generation of engineers. We participate in DD Robocon, India's most prestigious robotics competition, where the winning team earns the right to represent India at the ABU Asia Pacific Robotics Competition.

From circuit boards to autonomous systems, from CAD drawings to CNC machined parts — every skill matters. Our team combines mechanical excellence, electrical precision, and intelligent software to build robots that compete at the national level.
